### Risk: Assessment Gaming or Fraud

**Mitigation**:
- Process-focused assessment reduces gaming incentive
- Portfolio approach creates longitudinal evidence
- Reflection requirements reveal understanding
- Mix of AI- **Oral Defense**: Returning to viva voce assessment methods

### Intrinsic Motivation & "The Vibe Shift"
Policing is a losing battle. The only sustainable mitigation is a cultural shift (a **"Vibe Shift"**) where relying on AI to do the thinking is seen as "low status" or "boring." We foster an **"Honor Code 2.0"** co-written by students, emphasizing that the goal is *competence*, and faking competence only cheats oneself of the "superpower" of being an AI Director.
